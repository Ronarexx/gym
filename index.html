About Us

Our Fitness Center

Core Fitness Gym is the leading Health Club in Kampala Located in Ntinda on Ntinda Complex Block C roof top. We provide an enjoyable, yet efficient workout environment for our clients.

We accommodate everyone whether you’re a week day weights warrior, or simply looking to find a fun and engaging group training class to call your own, we’ve got a great range of fitness and wellness solutions to help you achieve your fitness and weight loss goals.

Core Fitness Gym is your home for personal training and group fitness classes with a light, airy atmosphere, premiere group fitness instructors and studios.
We have huge parking places, full ventilation and air-conditioning systems
